Quick info

Commander Zavala

The leader of the vangard who protect the last city on earth.

Universe:

Destiny

Race:

Awoken

Backstory

Zavala like most gaurdians was found by a ghost, not remembering his past. He then wandered around earth and found the last city, Zavala trained under Lord Saladin, eventually meeting Safiyah. Zavala and Safiyah later find an abondoned infant named Hakim, they both raise this child together. But sadly Hakim died at 15 after an attack from enemies called the fallen, Safiyah dies shortly after.

Personality

Zavala is stern and very serious, while also being scared. He does not like to send gaurdians on missions too far away form the last city, as he fears they will suffer the same fate as Hakim.
